About BloodSyno

BloodSyno is a platform to provide information about blood banks, blood availability, blood donation camps and assistant automation. More than 3800 blood centers are registered on BloodSyno from 29 states and 8 UTs.

BloodSyno is a citizen-centric portal and mobile app for various services such as nearest blood center, blood availability & blood donation camps information. It enforces blood centers to strictly follow national blood policy standards and guidelines.
